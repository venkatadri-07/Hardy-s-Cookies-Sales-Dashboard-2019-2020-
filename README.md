## Project Overview

The dashboard is designed to help understand:

Actionable recommendations are derived directly from the data insights.

---

## Key Metrics

- Total Sales  
- Total Profit  
- Total Orders  
- Average Purchase Value per Order  

---

## Key Insights

- Chocolate Chip cookies are the best-selling product with the highest profit margin  
- Fortune Cookies contribute significantly to sales but generate very low profit  
- Sales show noticeable drops during specific months (Feb, May, Aug, Nov)  
- Green Bay, Wisconsin contributes a major portion of total sales
